We're doing this primarily for ourselves.

The goal is to be around people who have high agency, are free-thinking, and are supported by structures that allow their ideas to become reality. Creative, driven people collaborating with each other with integrity to solve problems, create beautiful things, and lead fulfilling lives.

That's the world we wish to be part of. A world we're proud to be part of. And since it doesn't fully exist yet, we've decided to help build it.

---

## The two things that have to be true

For this to work, two things have to be in place.

The first is **Right Infrastructure**: peace in the country, good governance, good roads, electricity, internet, schools, hospitals, access to clean food, water, and Basic Freedoms including of speech, peaceful assembly, and of economic enterprise.

The second is **Prepared Human Resource**: Educated and Thinking Critically, Unafraid, Ready to Question Authority, Able to Spot Opportunity, and Willing to Work Hard.

The first side is largely outside our hands. The second side is where we work.

---

## What "prepared" actually means

Being prepared has two distinct dimensions.

The first: **World Class Employees (Hireability).** Solid, reliable, trustworthy, excellent at what they do, high integrity, able to think critically and creatively, able to see something through from inception to completion. World Class Hireability will bring immense wealth and knowledge to the country, a stepping stone to the next and more important stage.

The second: **World Beating Entrepreneurs (Inventiveness).** Awakened to their own true potential, eager to solve local problems with solutions best adapted for local needs, able to generate opportunity for themselves and nurture homegrown talent. World Beating Inventiveness will bolster our confidence in ourselves, make us more self-reliant in all spheres of life, and give us a seat at the global table as a leader and not a mere follower. Most importantly, it is this self-reliance that will buffer the workforce against the inevitable and disruptive changes that will come.

These aren't opposites. The best employees think like makers. The best entrepreneurs know how to execute. We're trying to cultivate both.

## What matters to us in this pursuit

<NeedsWheel />

We'd love to impact the lives of lakhs of students in India, through clever design and technology, not by increasing headcount. We don't have ambitions to be a large organisation in terms of team size. We wish to remain a small team that creates true and lasting transformation in society by teaching students.

This activity gives our lives meaning. Teaching young people skills to improve themselves is something we're genuinely passionate about, and we want the organisation to grow and maintain itself sustainably, financially for starters. The working style has to be slow and thoughtful.

And if we're not having fun doing this, we'll stop doing this.

## The vehicle

All of this runs through [Real World Ready](/real-world-ready).

It's the programme we've built to be the thing we needed when we were young. It's the programme we'd send people we love to. It's how we're making the world we want to live in a little more real.

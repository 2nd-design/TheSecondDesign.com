The second episode of Proof of Work is live: [Order the Chai Before You Get Permission](/proof-of-work/order-the-chai-before-you-get-permission), a conversation with Abina NJ.

Before she had permission to run the event, she'd already ordered 200 cups of chai and 200 puffs from a local bakery. Her principal said no anyway. Then said no again.

What she did to get from there to a full faculty meeting arguing her case, and what actually changed her principal's mind (it wasn't the pitch), is the whole episode. Watch it above, or read the full story on the episode page.
